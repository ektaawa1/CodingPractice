What is a prefix sum???
Array:  [1,  2,  3,  4,  5]
Index:   0   1   2   3   4

Prefix sums (running total):
After index 0: 1
After index 1: 1+2 = 3
After index 2: 1+2+3 = 6
After index 3: 1+2+3+4 = 10
After index 4: 1+2+3+4+5 = 15

So, Prefix sum at index i = sum of everything from index 0 to i.
========================================================================================

How to find the subarray sum (i to j index)??
The formula is Sum(i to j) = prefixSum(j) - prefixSum(i-1)
where the i-1 means "everything BEFORE the starting index of your subarray."

sum of any subarray = difference of two prefix sums.

For Example -
You want sum from index 2 to index 4 = 3+4+5 = 12
j = 4  (ending index)
i = 2  (starting index)

prefixSum[j]   = prefixSum[4] = 15  (sum of everything up to end)
prefixSum[i-1] = prefixSum[1] = 3   (sum of everything BEFORE start)

Answer = 15 - 3 = 12
